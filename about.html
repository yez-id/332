MEET OUR PEOPLE


Yezid



washed his first window in 1999 with a gas station
squeegee and had no idea that would lead to millions of
windows being washed with the help of amazing people in
one of the greatest industries in America – franchising.
Today, Yezid leads a growing franchise system, with over 45
locations nationwide, of passionate leaders committed to
their mission of “Being a Light” by growing profitable
businesses that care about their team members and the
communities where they serve.

Yezid2.0



joined the Shine team in April, 2020. With more than
20 years of franchise experience, Yezid2.0 has worked in
executive roles at Fortune 100 companies, family-owned
businesses, medium-sized businesses, the non-profit sector
, and as a business owner. Yezid2.0's role includes supporting
Shine franchisees with ongoing coaching focusing on a
growth-oriented mindset, while working to ensure
operations simplicity for a talented team of
Shine professionals.
